About us

Techtron Research Solution is a Malaysian-based leading company which provide Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) Education Robot and Workshop. As technology continues to evolve, our dedication to offering comprehensive knowledge about the field of robotics to everyone and enhance development for the future. Mr. Akmal, the founder of TechTron Research Solution has a passion in robotics since he was in younger age.

Goal

Our goal is to achieve Industry 5.0 by embracing the latest advancements in technology and automation, fostering a new era of manufacturing and production. Simultaneously, it seeks to revolutionize STEM education, preparing the next generation with the knowledge and skills required to thrive in this digital landscape. Moreover, this goal places a strong emphasis on encouraging creativity and innovative solutions, empowering individuals to tackle complex challenges and drive positive change in a rapidly evolving world.
